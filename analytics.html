How to Use ML Analytics

  1. Train Models: Click the "Train Models" button to train both Attrition and Performance prediction models using your current employee data. This process analyzes patterns and builds predictive models.
  2. Predict All: After training, use "Predict All" to run predictions on all employees at once. This will generate attrition risk scores and performance predictions.
  3. Individual Predictions: You can also predict for individual employees from the Employees page using the brain icon next to each employee.
  4. View Results: Check the Dashboard and Reports pages to see prediction results, trends, and insights. All predictions are saved to the history for audit trails.
Pro Tip:

Regularly retrain your models as you add more employee data to improve prediction accuracy. Look for patterns in high-risk departments and take proactive measures to improve employee retention.
